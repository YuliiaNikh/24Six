Features

Your family. Your values.
Your control.

Multimedia Library

Get more than music

Enjoy your favorite music, videos and podcasts. All in one app. With one subscription. And no unwanted material mixed in.

Custom Filters

Control your content

Add or remove content to suit your family’s standards. Create filters for different users. (So Sarah has her filter and Chaim has his.) You can choose to filter by female-only content, parenting and marriage topics and more.

Offline HD Streaming

Listen anytime, anywhere

Good news: You don’t need wifi to stream. Enjoy high-definition streaming quality for all your music, videos and podcasts — anywhere. In camp, Yeshiva, or on the road.

Personal Playlist and Libraries

Find your favorites fast

Curate playlists and libraries with your favorite songs for hours of uninterrupted listening. Hit play on your top picks with a tap.

Shared Playlists

Access popular, public playlists

Check out public playlists — 24Six’s playlists, an artist’s playlist, your friend’s playlist. Erev Shabbos? Hit play on Yaakov Shwekey’s Erev Shabbos Playlist. Exercising? Choose the Fast Jogging Playlist.

Video in Music

Switch seamlessly from music to video and back

Want to watch the music video of the song you’re playing? View the video in a heartbeat without interrupting your music — the video is embedded within the music.

In-App Notifications

Never miss a beat from your favorite artists

Enjoy to-the-minute updates when your favorite artists release new songs or your favorite podcast uploads new episodes. Ping!Yaakov Shwekey just released a new album.

Integrated Search

Explore multiple entertainment types with one search

Enjoy the most advanced search function available today. Search for anything and see everything, — music, videos podcasts, playlists and more. All in one place.

Smartlisten™

Discover more of what you love

Our AI learns what you like and suggests more content based on your preferences. Explore new horizons — within your comfort-level.

24Six Originals

Enjoy exclusive content

Get access to 24Six originals from popular Jewish artists. Songs, videos and podcasts you can’t find anywhere else.
